Exports of Peru's textile and clothing sector would total less than US $ 1.2 billion by the end of 2016, less than that reported in 2015 (US $ 1.3 billion), the Peruvian Exporters Association (Adex) reported. However, the export guild considered that although the figure will mean a new year of decline, the trend of exports of clothing indicates that they could start their recovery, or at least slow the fall. César Tello, chairman of the ADEX Clothing Committee, explained that this sector suffered a loss of competitiveness compared to other countries for three years in which there were consecutive declines in exports.

"Among the problems that are affecting the sector highlights the competition that brands have to buy from Peruvian companies"
